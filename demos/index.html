Boundary of this demo

  • Evidence-backed editorial answers are curated; no LLM is invoked at build time.
  • The showcase does not benchmark human productivity or generative answer quality.
  • Read-only MCP access does not authorize mutation of upstream resources.
  • Publisher license and attribution requirements still apply to source reuse.
